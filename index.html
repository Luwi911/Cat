Cold Cat Case

Instructions

You're a detective working a missing persons case: 2 civilians and the case's previous detective go missing. During your investigation, you meet a crazy-cat lady who seems to know where the victims went. She lures you inside, where she reveals herself to be a witch by turning you into a cat. Your new goal is to escape, and you meet 3 cats who on the way. You must work together to return to normal before it's too late.
