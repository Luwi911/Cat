Chapter 1

You are a detective, slaving away at your desk doing paperwork when you overhear a conversation between the captain and another detective about an old missing persons case. Apparently the last detective to work on it has also gone missing and it's been a year since then. The public is getting restless and so the captain is looking for someone else who would be up to the task in the previous detective’s absence.

Nobody else is willing to volunteer, but you spring at the opportunity to do so! Your whole reason for joining this agency was because you wanted to help the community, and lately you’ve been getting tired of the constant paperwork. Despite the unknown fate of the last detective and the case going cold, this may be your only chance to do something. By leading a case on your own, maybe, just maybe you can prove yourself to the captain.

You're given a case file containing images of the 3 missing people, including the detective. The only clue to their disappearance is their last known location; a forest situated by the edge of the town which is mostly abandoned. In haste, you set out to the forest for any potential leads.
