Chapter 4

Just as you're about to come to a boiling point, the witch takes her eyes off you for a moment, and you seize the opportunity to tip the cauldron, spilling the resurrection potion. While you're thankfully unscathed, you make sure to help the others who may need it. Suddenly, you all feel a shivering feeling so intensely you pass out. You regain consciousness and feel the damp grass on your skin. Wait, skin. Your eyes fly open to see human hands on the grass. You're human again. You look and are relieved that everyone has returned to normal, but a sudden shriek disrupts your victory: Before she can finish, there's a sudden scream of agony. You stare in horror as her veins burst through her skin and light seeps out of her mouth and eye sockets. You squeeze your own eyes shut; leaving the last of your senses exposed to her echoing screams dissipating into the blaring light. Everything suddenly goes quiet, but you still wait a few seconds, the image of her last moments burned in your mind. As you carefully readjust your vision, all you see is a steaming puddle of goo in the witch's place.

Half a year has passed since your first case. The residents of the witch's neighbourhood were turned back to human and readjusting back to their old lives. Despite your naïveté during your investigation, you still gained the appreciation of the previous detective, which helped you land a promotion from the Chief. Although the witch's fate is unfortunate, it's possible that she will have the opportunity to see her Sesame again in the afterlife.
